# Stats Central  
### Every Game. Every Stat. One Platform.

**Created by:** Abdulla Saleh, Mohamed Ali, and Ahnaf Abid

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Stats Central is a centralized sports betting intelligence platform that combines real-time stats, standings, player performance, and betting odds into one mobile app.

This project serves as a comprehensive case study in digital product development, covering:
- Market research
- User persona creation
- Competitive analysis
- Monetization strategy

---

## 🔍 The Problem Space

Through our market research, we identified a fractured user experience in the sports betting ecosystem.

Sports bettors often bounce between:
- ESPN
- Twitter/X
- Reddit
- Multiple sportsbook apps

...just to make one informed wager.

### Key Pain Points
- Gathering reliable data is slow and inconsistent
- Information becomes stale before games begin
- Users feel overwhelmed by fragmented sources
- Incomplete information leads to poor betting decisions

---

## ✨ Proposed Solution & Core Features

Stats Central brings live stats, standings, injuries, and betting odds into one unified platform.

### Core Features

#### 📊 Live Stats & Scores
Real-time game data across:
- NBA
- NFL
- MLB
- EPL

#### 🚑 Injury Reports
Up-to-date player availability and injury tracking.

#### 🏆 Standings & Rankings
Live conference and division standings updated continuously.

#### 💰 Betting Odds
Access to:
- Spreads

## 👥 User Personas & Journey

Our UX research focused on user archetypes within the 50M+ active sports bettors in the United States.

### Rick
**Age:** 22  
**Occupation:** Entry-level finance analyst  
**Location:** Manhattan, NYC

Rick wants to:
- Research games quickly (10–20 minutes)
- Make data-backed bets instead of gut decisions
- Avoid switching between multiple apps

**Frustration:** Stats are often outdated by the time he reads them.

---

### Mike
**Age:** 47  
**Occupation:** IT Manager  
**Location:** Jersey City, NJ

Mike primarily builds NFL parlays on Sundays.

He relies heavily on:
- Injury reports
- Line movement
- Matchup insights

**Goal:** Spot value bets before odds shift.
